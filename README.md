# 🎓 Student Performance Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes the academic and lifestyle habits of **1000 students** to understand the factors influencing examination performance.

The dashboard combines interactive visualizations with KPI cards to identify relationships between study habits, sleep patterns, diet quality, entertainment usage, and exam scores.

---



## Dataset

- 1000 student records
- Academic performance
- Study hours
- Sleep hours
- Netflix usage
- Social media usage
- Diet quality
- Gender
- Parental education
- Extracurricular participation

---

## Business Questions Answered

- Average Netflix and Social Media usage
- Average sleeping hours
- Does sleep affect exam scores?
- Top-performing student segments
- Impact of study hours on exam score
- Average score by gender
- Extracurricular participation analysis
- Parental education analysis
- Effect of diet quality on exam score
- Outlier analysis of high Netflix users scoring above average

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Visualization

---

## Key Insights

- Study hours positively correlate with exam performance.
- Students with better diet quality generally score higher.
- Sleep duration shows noticeable variation across score groups.
- High Netflix usage does not necessarily imply poor academic performance, with several outliers performing above average.
- Gender-wise comparison highlights differences in extracurricular participation and average scores.
